Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This privacy policy describes how touseef-automation ("the app", "we", "this tool") accesses, uses, and stores data obtained through your LinkedIn account. This app is a personal automation tool built and operated by Syed Touseef for the purpose of scheduling and publishing LinkedIn posts on his own behalf.

What this app does

touseef-automation is a content automation tool that generates LinkedIn post drafts, creates accompanying images, and — only after manual human review and approval — publishes those posts to a single, specific LinkedIn account that the app owner controls.

Data we access

When you authorize this app through LinkedIn's OAuth flow, it requests the following permissions:

  • Basic profile information (via OpenID Connect: openid, profile) — used only to confirm which LinkedIn account is authorizing the app and to resolve the member identifier needed to publish posts.
  • Post on your behalf (w_member_social) — used to publish approved post content and an accompanying image to your LinkedIn feed.

This app does not request or access your connections list, messages, email address, employer data, or any data belonging to other LinkedIn members.

How we use this data

  • Your LinkedIn member identifier is used solely to direct API calls to your own account when publishing approved posts.
  • No data obtained from LinkedIn is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.
  • No data obtained from LinkedIn is used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing the posts you have personally reviewed and approved.

Data storage

Authentication tokens (access token and refresh token) are stored locally in a private repository and/or private environment variables controlled by the app owner. They are not stored in any publicly accessible location. Generated post drafts and images are stored temporarily in a private Google Sheet and local/private cloud storage solely for the purpose of human review before publishing. No LinkedIn profile data is retained beyond what is needed to operate the app.

Human review before publishing

No content is posted to LinkedIn automatically without review. Every post generated by this app is held in a pending state until manually approved by the app owner. This app does not post, like, comment, or take any other action on LinkedIn without that explicit, manual approval step.

Third-party services involved

This app uses the following third-party services to function. Each handles data under its own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic / OpenAI — used to generate post text and images. No LinkedIn profile data is sent to these services; only generic content topics are sent.
  • Google Sheets — used as a private review queue for draft posts before publishing. Access is restricted to the app owner's Google account.
  • LinkedIn API — used to publish approved content to the app owner's own LinkedIn account.

Data retention and deletion

You may revoke this app's access at any time from your LinkedIn account settings under "Permitted Services." Revoking access immediately invalidates the app's ability to post on your behalf. To request deletion of any locally stored data tied to your account, contact us using the details below.

Changes to this policy

If this app's data practices change, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
